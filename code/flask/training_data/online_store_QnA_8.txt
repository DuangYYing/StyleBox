How to change my profile information?
You can  click on "Account" and then open your profile. You can also click "Eidt" to edit the information.
How to change my account information?
You can click on "Account" and then click "Account Setting" to edit the information.
How to view my followed stylists?
You can click on "Account" and then click "Follwed Stylists" to view the list of stylists.
How to view all stylists?
You can click on "Stylists" on the homepage and then you can get the list of stylists. You can also search stylists by filters.
How to search stylists?
You can click on "Stylists" on the homepage. Then you can search by keyword or filter by sort options or style options.
